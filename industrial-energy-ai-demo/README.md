# Industrial Energy Efficiency AI Demo
This project is a lightweight AI-driven demo for analyzing industrial equipment energy efficiency and operational risk, applicable to energy, semiconductor manufacturing, and heavy industrial systems.
Industrial facilities face increasing pressure to reduce energy consumption, predict equipment risks, and optimize operational efficiency.
This demo shows how data-driven analysis can be used as a foundation for predictive maintenance and energy optimization systems.
## What This Demo Does

- Analyzes equipment load, temperature, and runtime
- Estimates energy consumption patterns
- Identifies high-load operational risk indicators
- Can be extended to predictive maintenance and AI optimization models
## Applicable Industries

- Energy & Power Systems
- Semiconductor Manufacturing Equipment
- Industrial Machinery & Automation
- Smart Factories / Industry 4.0
## Business Value

- Helps identify inefficiencies in industrial energy usage
- Reduces operational risk through early data analysis
- Serves as a prototype for AI-driven industrial monitoring platforms
- Suitable for rapid MVP demonstrations and investor presentations
## Future Roadmap

- Machine learning-based energy prediction
- Equipment failure risk modeling
- Integration with IoT sensor data
